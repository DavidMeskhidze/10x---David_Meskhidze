ALEXANDER VOLKANOVSKI

 THE GREAT


Career

  • 2018 - made his UFC debut at UFC Fight Night 101 in Melbourne, defeating Yusuke Kasuya via TKO in the second round. This marked his entry into the world's top MMA promotion​
  • 2019 - He defeated Max Holloway at UFC 245 via unanimous decision to become the UFC Featherweight Champion, a pivotal moment in his career​
  • 2020 - Volkanovski successfully defended his featherweight title in a rematch against Max Holloway at UFC 251, winning via split decision
  • 2021 - In one of the most intense fights of his career, he defeated Brian Ortega at UFC 266 via unanimous decision, retaining his title after surviving two tight submission
  • 2022 - Volkanovski faced Max Holloway for the third time at UFC 276, winning convincingly via unanimous decision and solidifying his dominance in the featherweight division​
  • 2023- Volkanovski moved up to lightweight to challenge Islam Makhachev for the UFC Lightweight Championship at UFC 284. Despite a valiant effort, he lost via unanimous decision
  • 2023- He successfully defended his featherweight title against Yair Rodríguez at UFC 290, winning via TKO in the third round
  • 2024 - El Matador ☯
