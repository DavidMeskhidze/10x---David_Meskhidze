Facts about Margot:

  • 1990 - Born Dalby, Queensland, Australia
  • She was enrolled in a circus school by her mother, where she excelled in trapeze
  • Robbie's first acting roles came when she was in high school. She starred in two low-budget independent thriller films, called Vigilante and I.C.U.
  • She made her television debut in a 2008 guest role as Caitlin Brentford in the drama series City Homicide and followed this with a two-episode arc in the children's television series The Elephant Princess
  • From 2008-2010 - Robbie played the character of Donna Freedman in the long-running Australian soap opera, Neighbours.
